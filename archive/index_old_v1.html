Impact

Product Strategy and Risk Analytics
Optimized Product Pricing
I led an 8-member team to design and implement a cutting-edge pricing algorithm for a $2B disability product. By leveraging predictive models (XGBoost & GLMs) and competitor data, we improved risk rating and selection, which reduced underwriting cycles and enhanced market competitiveness, ultimately leading to wider and easier product adoption.

Reduced Future Liabilities
I led a team of three data scientists in developing data-driven risk models (Poisson GLM). These models, which predict claim occurrence and termination, enabled us to estimate a reduction of approximately $600 million in future liabilities for a sensitive portfolio valued at over $15 billion. My work included identifying emerging risk patterns through SHAP analysis.
Operational Analytics
Boosted Quote Efficiency
I developed a data-driven risk-rating model (GBM, GLM) that boosted quote efficiency by 300% for long-term disability insurance. This solution was scaled across four additional product lines, demonstrating a significant improvement in operational efficiency.

Streamlined Claims Processing
I developed a survival model to predict claim duration, which enabled the strategic prioritization of open claims for handling and approval. This solution significantly reduced manual effort and streamlined the claims examination process.

Fraud Detection
I engineered an anomaly detection framework (using isolation forest, CADE) to identify fraudulent financial transactions, enabling more efficient compliance communication.
